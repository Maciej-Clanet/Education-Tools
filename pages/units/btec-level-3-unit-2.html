Unit Overview

What this unit covers

This unit focuses on the fundamental principles of how computer systems work, including the role of hardware and software, the way components interact, and how data is stored, transmitted, and used.

Why it matters

Understanding how computer systems behave helps with technical support, software development, troubleshooting, and choosing efficient solutions in real computing roles.
