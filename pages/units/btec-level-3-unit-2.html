Unit Overview

What this Unit 2 revision hub covers

This unit focuses on the fundamental principles of how computer systems work, including the role of hardware and software, the way components interact, and how data is stored, represented, transmitted, and used. The written exam expects students to move confidently between definitions, applied scenarios, and technical comparisons.

Why it matters

Understanding how computer systems behave helps with technical support, software development, troubleshooting, and choosing efficient solutions in real computing roles.
